Group 7 | Assignment 3

DATABASE: gonature_db
ENGINE   : MySQL 8.x
CHARSET  : utf8mb4 / utf8mb4_unicode_ci

========================================
TABLE OVERVIEW
========================================

1. park
   Purpose : Configuration and real-time state of each national park.
   Columns :
     park_id              – Primary key (auto-increment)
     name                 – Park display name
     max_capacity         – Maximum simultaneous visitors allowed
     casual_gap           – Number of slots reserved for walk-in (casual) visitors;
                            pre-booked orders are capped at (max_capacity – casual_gap)
     estimated_stay_time  – Average visit duration in hours; used for overlap detection
                            when checking whether a new booking conflicts with existing ones
     current_visitors     – Live count of visitors currently inside the park; incremented
                            on entry, decremented on exit
     active_discount      – Park-wide promotional discount in percent (0 = no discount);
                            applied on top of the order-type pricing rules
   Rows at seed : 8 parks (Banias, Carmel, Galilee, Negev, Masada, Caesarea, Timna, Nahal Ayun)

2. visitor
   Purpose : Stores every person who has ever booked or entered a park.
   Columns :
     visitor_id   – National ID number (9 chars, PK); walk-ins receive a synthetic
                    "WI<timestamp>" ID that never conflicts with real IDs
     first_name   – First name (default: 'Guest')
     last_name    – Last name (default: 'Visitor')
     email        – Contact email (default: 'Not Provided')
     phone        – Mobile phone (default: 'Not Provided')
     visitor_type – 'Regular' | 'Subscriber' | 'Guide'
   Rows at seed : 3 sample visitors

3. employee
   Purpose : Staff accounts used for login and role-based access.
   Columns :
     employee_id – Primary key (auto-increment)
     username    – Unique login username
     password    – Password (plain text for course demo; production would use hashing)
     first_name  – Employee first name
     last_name   – Employee last name
     email       – Employee email
     role        – 'Park Manager' | 'Dept Manager' | 'Gate Worker' | 'Service Rep'
     park_id     – FK to park; NULL for Department Manager (oversees all parks)
   Rows at seed : 13 employees (1 Dept Manager, 8 Park Managers, 2 Gate Workers, 2 Service Reps)

4. subscriber
   Purpose : Members and certified tour guides (a subset of visitors with extra privileges).
   Columns :
     subscriber_id – Auto-increment member number (this is the card number shown to the user)
     visitor_id    – FK to visitor; the visitor must already exist
     family_size   – Maximum group size for family/group bookings
     credit_card   – Credit card number (used for membership billing)
     is_guide      – 1 = certified tour guide, 0 = regular subscriber
   Notes :
     - Subscribers receive a 10% loyalty discount on pre-booked orders.
     - Guides receive a 25% + 12% group discount; the guide themselves enters free.
   Rows at seed : 2 sample subscribers (1 regular, 1 guide)

5. visit_order
   Purpose : Every booking in the system (pre-planned and walk-in).
   Columns :
     order_id          – Primary key (auto-increment, printed on ticket)
     park_id           – FK to park
     visitor_id        – FK to visitor
     visit_date        – Date of the visit (DATE)
     visit_time        – Arrival time window (TIME, HH:MM:SS)
     visitor_count     – Number of visitors in this booking
     order_type        – 'Personal' | 'Family' | 'Group'
     status            – Lifecycle state (see status flow below)
     price             – Calculated price in ILS at the time of booking
     email             – Booker's email (copied from visitor at booking time for notifications)
     phone             – Booker's phone (copied from visitor at booking time for SMS)
     entry_timestamp   – When visitors actually entered the park (NULL until entry)
     exit_timestamp    – When visitors exited (NULL until exit)
     created_at        – When the order was placed (used to guard the 24h reminder logic)
     notification_time – When the 24h reminder was sent; NULL = not yet sent
   Status flow :
     Pre-booked  → Booked → [24h before] Pending Confirm → [confirmed] Confirmed → In Park → Completed
     Full park   → Waitlisted → [slot freed] Waitlist Pending → [confirmed] Confirmed → In Park → Completed
     Terminal    : Cancelled (manual or 2h timeout) | Expired (waitlist 1h timeout)
   Walk-in orders skip the Booked/Waitlisted phases; they are inserted directly as 'In Park'.
   Rows at seed : 3 sample orders

6. parameter_request
   Purpose : Park Manager requests to change park capacity, casual gap, or stay time.
             Must be approved by the Department Manager before taking effect.
   Columns :
     request_id               – Primary key (auto-increment)
     park_id                  – FK to park
     new_max_capacity         – Proposed maximum capacity
     new_casual_gap           – Proposed casual (walk-in) gap
     new_estimated_stay_time  – Proposed average stay time in hours
     status                   – 'Pending' | 'Approved' | 'Denied'
   Rows at seed : 0 (populated at runtime)

7. park_promotion
   Purpose : Park Manager requests for a temporary promotional discount.
             Must be approved or cancelled by the Department Manager.
   Columns :
     promotion_id     – Primary key (auto-increment)
     park_id          – FK to park
     park_name        – Denormalized park name (for display without JOIN)
     discount_percent – Proposed discount in percent (1–100)
     status           – 'Pending' | 'Approved' | 'Denied' | 'Cancelled'
     created_at       – When the request was submitted
   Rows at seed : 0 (populated at runtime)

========================================
PRICING RULES (applied by DBController.processNewOrder)
========================================

Base ticket price : 100 ILS per person

Pre-booked Personal / Family:
  - All visitors         : -15% (advance booking discount)
  - + Subscriber         : additional -10%

Pre-booked Group (requires certified guide):
  - Paying visitors      : base * 0.75 * 0.88  (guide enters free)
  - + Subscriber guide   : additional -10%

Walk-in Personal / Family:
  - Full price (100 ILS/person)

Walk-in Group:
  - 10% off full price

Park-level promotional discount:
  - Applied multiplicatively on top of any of the above rules

========================================
AUTOMATED NOTIFICATION ENGINE
========================================

A background thread (ScheduledExecutorService, 1-minute tick) runs inside DBController:

Phase 1 – 24h Reminders:
  - Selects Booked orders whose visit time is exactly 24h away.
  - Updates status → 'Pending Confirm', stamps notification_time.
  - Sends confirmation email (with clickable Confirm / Cancel links) and SMS.
  - If the visitor does not confirm within 2 hours → Phase 2.

Phase 2 – Auto-Cancel:
  - Selects 'Pending Confirm' orders where notification_time ≤ NOW() – 2h.
  - Cancels them; triggers waitlist promotion.

Phase 3 – Waitlist Expiry:
  - Selects 'Waitlist Pending' orders where notification_time ≤ NOW() – 1h.
  - Cancels them; triggers next waitlist promotion.

Waitlist Promotion:
  - When a slot frees up, the oldest 'Waitlisted' order that fits is promoted
    to 'Waitlist Pending' and the visitor receives an email + SMS alert.
